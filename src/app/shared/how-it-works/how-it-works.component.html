How To Use

Welcome to the user guide for training the AI Database. Below, you'll find explanations for the types and formats of queries developers need to train the AI Database effectively. This system is designed to surpass basic search features by providing you with useful results to support more informed decision-making. However, to achieve this level of performance, the system must be trained accordingly.

Training Queries

Your queries can encompass a range of topics, including frequently asked questions about existing database information. By training the AI to recognize and respond to these queries, you can enhance efficiency and streamline collaboration processes among team members.

Examples of Training Scenarios

  • Retaining Answers: Train the AI to retain answers to questions that you regularly encounter, enabling quicker access to essential information.
  • Operational Efficiency: Input commonly requested information to facilitate smoother operation and workflow within your organization.
  • Collaboration Enhancement: Use the AI to streamline collaboration processes between team members by providing access to shared knowledge and resources.

Additional Information Required

In addition to the training queries, please provide the name and email of any other staff member to validate their authorized access to the system. This information will enable us to prepare for testing interface navigation and functionality effectively. We will update you on the actual testing date once the necessary preparations are complete.
